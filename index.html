üìù System Summary

iSeed is an autonomous agricultural robot that detects suitable soil, aligns using a line-following sensor, and plants seeds using a rack-and-pinion mechanism. Our goal was to develop a sustainable and low-cost method for precision planting in small gardens and greenhouses.
